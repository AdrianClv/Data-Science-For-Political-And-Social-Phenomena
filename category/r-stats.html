Applied Social Good: Data Science, Political Science, & History

I am a data scientist and political scientist

After years working on technology projects in humanitarian aid and international development, I cofounded Popily, a startup to democratize data science, and co-host a general audience data science podcast.

This site is my repository of 164 articles and tutorials on data science, statistics, and computational social science. For more details, see GitHub.
